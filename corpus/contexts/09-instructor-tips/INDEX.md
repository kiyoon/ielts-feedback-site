## How to use these files (LLM-marker context)

When marking a learner essay aimed at band 7.0+, the most leveraged interventions in priority order:

1. **Paraphrase the prompt nouns** (file 01 / 02). A single repeated keyword from the prompt can keep the candidate stuck at band 6.0.
2. **Vary the sentence subject** (file 04). Passive voice and `it is X that…` structures double the apparent grammatical range.
3. **Pair every comparative with a hedging adverb** (file 05). "Higher" alone is band 6; "considerably higher" is band 7.
4. **Drop in one inversion or cleft per essay** (file 07). One `Not only…but…` or `Only by…can…` is enough to push GRA up half a band.
5. **Use cohesion that is not Firstly/Secondly** (file 08). Mechanical linkers cap C&C at 6.5.
6. **Audit for L1 errors** (file 10). For Korean/Chinese writers, articles and plural-marking errors alone block band 7.
