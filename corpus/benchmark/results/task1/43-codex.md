Band 7 requires more precision, for example "rose overall", "the figure for the USA", "throughout the period", and "followed a contrasting pattern" (`contexts/09-instructor-tips/01-paraphrase-banks-task1.md`, `contexts/09-instructor-tips/03-trend-language-bank.md`).

Meaning is rarely blocked, so Band 6 is fair; Band 7 would need frequent error-free sentences and better control of past-tense line-graph reporting (`contexts/08-vocabulary-grammar/01-grammar-error-correction-bank.md`).
