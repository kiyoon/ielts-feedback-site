## Question Types Reference

[`question-types-overview.md`](./question-types-overview.md) covers the six core Task 2 question types with example prompts and structural guidance:
1. Opinion essays ("Agree/Disagree", "To what extent…")
2. Discuss both views + give your own opinion
3. Advantages and disadvantages (with sub-type 3b: "do the advantages outweigh…")
4. Problem and solution (cause + solution)
5. Two-part questions (multi-question prompts)
6. Positive or negative development / trend evaluation

It also includes a side-by-side comparison table and a 3-step check for identifying the question type.

## Notes on Reliability

- Essays from engnovate.com and ieltsbuddy.com have per-criterion scores and full essay text. The British Council PDF is the most authoritative.
- Some essays are reproduced verbatim where retrieved as plain text; a few were summarised by the fetch tool — these are flagged with a "[summarised]" note.
- Engnovate scoring is AI-assisted; treat per-criterion sub-scores as approximations, but the overall band tends to align with examiner expectations.
