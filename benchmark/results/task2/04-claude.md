## Per-criterion justification

**Task Response (6.0).** The prompt is an opinion essay ("To what extent do you agree or disagree?") and the candidate adopts a balanced position: *"I believe good balance of both is needed."* This is a relevant position (Band 6 floor) but the structure is a Discuss-Both-Views (Body 1 = computers help reading; Body 2 = handwriting helps writing) rather than a defence of the balance itself, so coverage is uneven and the conclusion repeats the intro thesis rather than synthesising — exactly the Band 6 pattern described in `contexts/01-band-descriptors/task2-band-descriptors.md` ("conclusions drawn may be unclear, unjustified or repetitive"). Body 2 is only three short sentences with no concrete example — *"writing skills need to be improved by lots of handwritten works"* is asserted, not developed. Per `contexts/05-band-6-to-7/task-response-upgrades.md` §10, this "underdeveloped main idea" is the single most common 6→7 leak.

**Coherence and Cohesion (6.0).** Four paragraphs with a clear progression — but the cohesion is mechanical and at one point inaccurate. *"On the other hands"* is a collocation error (singular *hand*) flagged in `contexts/08-vocabulary-grammar/03-cohesion-error-correction-bank.md` §2. *"Therefore, computers can play positive role"* is a wrong-relation linker — *therefore* implies a logical entailment the body has not actually established. The introduction is six sentences, four of which restate the same idea (*"Children are born into the digital world. From young age, they know how to operate computers... It is part of their daily life. School age children is no exception"*) — a clear case of "paragraphing not always logical" (`contexts/01-band-descriptors/task2-band-descriptors.md` §Band 6). Reference and substitution are absent: *computers* is repeated 14 times where `contexts/05-band-6-to-7/cohesion-upgrades.md` §3 would expect *they / these devices / such tools / digital tools*.

**Lexical Resource (7.0, upper edge).** The candidate reaches for some Band-7 items: *"utilise the computers"*, *"confident users of computers"*, *"come up with"*, *"enhance students' reading and writing skills"* — these are awareness-of-style markers per the Band 7 descriptor. However, multiple word-form / spelling / countability slips push this against the band's ceiling: *"the unknow words"* (spelling), *"On the other hands"* (collocation), *"lots of handwritten works"* (*work* uncountable per `contexts/09-instructor-tips/00-user-shared-feedback-2.md` §5), *"autocorrection"* (non-standard — usual term is *autocorrect*). **Grammatical Range & Accuracy (6.0).** A mix of simple and complex sentences (relative clauses present: *"computers that students are so used to"*) but article omissions and SVA errors are systematic, not occasional. *"School age children is"* (SVA — `contexts/08-vocabulary-grammar/01-grammar-error-correction-bank.md` §2), *"From young age"* (missing article), *"lead to decline"* (article), *"good balance of both is needed"* (article), *"play positive role"* (article), *"teacher should not avoid the use of computer"* (number agreement).
