## Structural feedback
You address both views and give an opinion, but your opinion shifts away from the prompt. The question asks whether parents or schools are better placed to teach social skills; your thesis says “it is up to individuals,” which answers a related but different question. Body 1 contains both the parent view and the school view, so neither side is developed as a full PEEL paragraph. Body 2 mostly repeats the “up to individuals” idea instead of weighing parents against schools. The conclusion also restates this off-centre position rather than synthesising the two views. The single highest-impact fix here is splitting Body 1 into two body paragraphs and rewriting your opinion as “parents lay the foundation, schools provide practice,” not adding fancier vocabulary.
