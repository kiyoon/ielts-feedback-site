**Coherence and Cohesion:** The essay has a logical four-paragraph structure: introduction, overview, major developed countries, then India. Several collocations are non-native: “had a growth”, “the figure of USA”, “all over the period”, and “had reach to”. Band 6 is appropriate because meaning remains clear despite restricted range and imprecise word choice (`01-band-descriptors/task1-academic-band-descriptors.md` §Band 6; `05-band-6-to-7/vocabulary-upgrades.md` §1).
