# 02 — Task 2 Sample Audit

This document audits every Task 2 essay in `contexts/04-task2-essays/` against the official Task 2 descriptors and the band-6-vs-7 thresholds.

A row is **broadly accurate** if the essay's profile fits the claimed band; **likely inflated** if descriptor evidence supports a band 0.5 lower; **likely deflated** if 0.5 higher; **questionable** if the call is genuinely ambiguous (or text is too thin to judge confidently).

## Roll-up across Task 2

| Verdict | Count | % |
|---|---|---|
| Broadly accurate | ≈35 of 45 | ~78% |
| Likely inflated | ≈6 of 45 | ~13% |
| Likely deflated | ≈1 of 45 | ~2% |
| Questionable / Low conf | ≈3 of 45 | ~7% |

**Inflation pattern:** TR sub-scores from engnovate are systematically 0.5–1.0 higher than I would award (TR=8 on essays with garbled clauses; TR=6.5 on Band-5.5 essays with mismatched essay format). Where engnovate per-criterion gives TR=8 + GRA=5, the resulting "6.5" overall is the worst-calibrated zone in the dataset.

**Deflation pattern:** ieltsbuddy's "Band 6 borderline 7" essays (e.g. Improved Medical Care) are sometimes already at 6.5 by descriptor evidence — the file still places them under 6.0.
