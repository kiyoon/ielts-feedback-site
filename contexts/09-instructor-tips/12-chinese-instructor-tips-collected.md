## 1. 知乎 — 雅思写作如何上 7 分

🔗 https://www.zhihu.com/question/29125535

**Original (synthesised from search summaries):** "雅思写作的7分标准是 'uses paraphrase effectively'（有效地使用转述/同义替换），这表明掌握同义词替换对达到7分至关重要。在雅思写作中，同一个词不要出现多次，否则就会被认为词汇多样性不足，导致评分比较低。"

**Translation:** "The IELTS Writing band-7 standard is 'uses paraphrase effectively'. This means mastering synonym substitution is essential to reach band 7. In IELTS Writing, no single word should appear multiple times, otherwise it will be judged as lacking lexical variety, leading to a lower score."

**Takeaway:** The Chinese cram-school rule — at band 7, **a content word should not repeat in a paragraph**. Topic-keyword repetition (`environment` 5 times) is the single biggest band-6 marker.

## 5. 新航道 — 雅思写作高分模板：强调与倒装

🔗 https://qd.xhd.cn/ielts/ysxiezuo/568710.html

**Excerpt:** "倒装共分两类：全部倒装和部分倒装。全部倒装是指将全部谓语动词都放在主语之前，部分倒装是指将谓语的一部分如助动词、系动词或情态动词放在主语之前。"

**Translation:** "Inversion has two types: full inversion and partial inversion. Full inversion places the entire verb before the subject; partial inversion places only a part of the verb (auxiliary, copula, or modal) before the subject."

**Recommended showcases (from this article):**
- "Sitting in front of the screen does damage the eye." (do-emphasis)
- "It is the government that should shoulder responsibility." (cleft)
- "Convenient as tele-education is, it cannot replace traditional education." (concessive inversion)
- "Only in this way can we deal with unemployment." (only + inversion)

**Takeaway:** Xinhang Dao's recommended **one cleft + one inversion per essay** — the same advice surfaces from Korean and Vietnamese sources.

## 6. 知乎 — 雅思小作文替换词

🔗 https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37521466

**Excerpt (synthesised):** "雅思写作中的替换表达不仅仅是同义词那么简单，更不是中文翻译相同就能相互替换，你需要具体地了解这个词适用的场景，才能准确地在文中使用出来。"

**Translation:** "Synonym substitution in IELTS Writing is not just synonyms — and certainly not 'same Chinese translation = interchangeable'. You need to know the specific context where each word is used to apply it accurately."

**Takeaway:** Zhihu's most-quoted warning: 同义词 ≠ 可互换 (synonyms ≠ interchangeable). E.g., `lethal / fatal / deadly / mortal` all translate to 致命的 in Chinese, but only `lethal` collocates with `weapon`, while `fatal` collocates with `mistake / error`.

## 7. 新航道 — 雅思写作中国考生常见错误

🔗 https://www.xhd.cn/ielts/xiezuo/143923.html

**Direct excerpt:** "考生写作使用可数名词时，不注意分辨自己是否使用了恰当的单复数形式。可数名词使用时一定要区分是使用单数名词还是复数名词，如果是单数名词，需在单数名词前加上冠词 a/an 或 the。"

**Translation:** "When writers use countable nouns, they often fail to distinguish whether they have used the correct singular/plural form. With countable nouns, you must distinguish singular from plural; if singular, you must place an article (a/an or the) before it."

**Takeaway:** Xinhang Dao's #1 error category for Chinese candidates is **article omission with countable singulars**. See file 10 §A for full pattern bank.

## 8. mypitaya — 中国雅思考生最易犯的十个语法错误

🔗 https://www.mypitaya.com/100505.html

**Top 5 errors (from extracted content):**

1. **Double verbs (双谓语)** — "There are many ways can improve students' English." → "There are many ways **which can** improve …"
2. **Missing articles** — "offers you chance" → "offers you **a** chance"
3. **"To" as preposition** — "In addition to **give** an introduction" → "In addition to **giving** …"
4. **Comma splice** — Two independent clauses joined by comma alone.
5. **Subject-verb agreement** — "Buying things make me happy" → "Buying things **makes** me happy"

**Takeaway:** Pitaya's #1 fix is **the double-verb error after `there be`** — endemic to L1 Chinese writers.

## 9. Spiiker — 雅思写作 7 种语法错误

🔗 https://m.spiiker.com/study-res/writing/111492580.html

**Selected error patterns with corrections:**

| 错误类型 | Wrong | Right |
|---|---|---|
| 双谓语 | "there were 80% students study for career" | "80% students **who studied** for career" |
| 主系表 | "We are impossible to make any progress" | "**It is impossible for us** to make progress" |
| 介词连接错误 | "using new energy to **instead of** the traditional" | "using new energy **to replace** traditional" |
| 标点错误 (comma vs full stop) | "weekly basis. Because it offers" | "weekly basis, because it offers" |

**Takeaway:** Spiiker's emphasis on the **"It is + adj + for sb + to V"** pattern — Chinese writers often mistakenly say "We are impossible / convenient / necessary".

## 10. 学为贵 — 超好用的雅思写作连接词

🔗 https://ielts.guixue.com/article/3990.shtml

**Excerpt:** "连接词是使段落中语句表达连贯的最主要方法。使用过渡词不仅可以使语句之间的逻辑关系更明确，还可以使文章看上去更完整紧密。"

**Translation:** "Connectives are the primary tool for making sentences in a paragraph cohesive. Using transition words not only clarifies the logical relationships between sentences but also makes the essay look more complete and tightly-knit."

**Recommended categories:**
- **因果 (cause-effect):** because, since, as, so, therefore, thus, as a result
- **转折 (contrast):** however, but, yet, on the other hand, in contrast
- **递进 (addition):** furthermore, moreover, in addition, besides, also
- **对比 (compare):** whereas, while, compared to, on the contrary
- **条件 (condition):** if, unless, until, provided that, in case

## 12. 知乎 — 雅思大作文模版大全

🔗 https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/440982337

**Synthesised:** "雅思大作文有3种文章结构：一边倒结构、折中型结构和让步反驳型结构，无论立场是支持还是反对，都可以写出不同的文章结构。"

**Translation:** "There are 3 essay structures for IELTS Task 2: one-sided argument, balanced/compromise, and concession-rebuttal. Regardless of whether your position supports or opposes, you can write different essay structures."

**Recommended template for two-view discussion:**

```
"One of the most controversial issues today relates to … While some people tend
towards the viewpoint that …, others support the idea that … In this essay, I
am going to examine both points of view and provide my own opinion."
```

**Takeaway:** The **concession-rebuttal structure** (acknowledge counter-view, then refute) is the Chinese cram-school's preferred structure for one-sided agree/disagree essays.

## 13. 知乎 — 一个 Introduction 应对四大题型

🔗 https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/110710097

**Synthesised:** "好的雅思大作文开头应该是漏斗形结构（funnel-shaped intro），由三部分组成：Background（背景）、Paraphrase topic（改写题目）、Outline（表明立场），从介绍背景逐步缩小范围到表明立场。"

**Translation:** "A good IELTS Task 2 introduction should be funnel-shaped (漏斗形), with three parts: Background, Paraphrase of the topic, and Outline of position — narrowing from general context to specific stance."

**Takeaway:** Chinese cram-school's **canonical funnel intro** (file 06 §1). Three sentences only — anything longer wastes word count.

## 14. 沪江 — 雅思小作文各题型技能总结

🔗 https://liuxue.hujiang.com/ielts/writing/p376573/

**Trend description bank from this source:**

```
增长 (increase): increase / rise / go up / climb / ascend / rocket
下降 (decrease): decrease / drop / slide / decline / plunge
波动 (fluctuate): fluctuate / rebound / undulate / wave
稳定 (stable): remain stable / stabilize / level off
```

**Five sentence patterns:**

```
1. Subject + 趋势动词 + 副词
2. There was a + 形容词 + 趋势名词 + in + 描述对象
3. 时间 + saw/experienced/witnessed + a + 形容词 + 趋势名词
4. A + 形容词 + 趋势名词 + took place/occurred
5. (Passive) A + 趋势名词 + was witnessed/observed in …
```

**Takeaway:** Hujiang's 5 patterns are the **canonical Chinese Task 1 sentence-pattern list**. Drop **at least 3 different patterns** in any Task 1 essay to push GRA to 7.0.
