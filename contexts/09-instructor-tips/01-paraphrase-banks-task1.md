## 1. The opening verb — "shows / illustrates"

🇨🇳 instructor rule (zim.vn / vietop.edu.vn): never start with "The graph shows…" if you have alternatives. The opening sentence is the first impression on the examiner; an unparaphrased opener already signals band 6.

| Common (band 6) | Upgrade (band 7+) |
|---|---|
| shows | illustrates / depicts / demonstrates / presents / outlines / sets out |
| gives information about | provides data on / supplies information regarding / offers data concerning |
| compares | draws a comparison between / contrasts / juxtaposes |
| The graph shows | The provided / supplied / given line graph illustrates |
| The chart shows | The presented bar chart demonstrates |
| The table shows | The supplied table outlines / The given table sets out |
| The diagram shows | The illustration depicts / The figure presents |

**Frame template (CN 新航道):** `The given / supplied / presented + [diagram type] + [verb] + data on + [topic] + [time period].`

```
- The given line graph illustrates data on energy consumption in four nations between 2000 and 2020.
- The presented pie chart depicts the proportion of household income spent on six categories in the UK in 2018.
- The supplied bar chart contrasts the percentages of male and female workers in five industries.
- The figure outlines how energy was distributed across sectors over a thirty-year period.
```

## 2. "People" / "the number of people"

| Common | Upgrade |
|---|---|
| people | citizens / residents / individuals / inhabitants / dwellers |
| people who live in X | individuals who **dwell in / reside in** X |
| the number of people | the figure for / the proportion of / the percentage of citizens |
| many people | a substantial proportion of / a significant share of / a large body of |
| few people | a small minority of / a marginal portion of / a negligible number of |
| young people | adolescents / youngsters / the younger generation / juveniles |
| old people | the elderly / senior citizens / the aged population |
| children | offspring / minors / the young (rare in Task 1) |
| workers | the workforce / labour force / employees / personnel |
| students | pupils / undergraduates / learners / scholars |

🇰🇷 (해커스): a Korean test-prep canon — replace "people" only **once per paragraph** with `citizens / residents`; over-replacement reads as awkward.

## 3. Population / demographic vocabulary (extends the user-shared table)

| Common | Upgrade |
|---|---|
| population | demographic figure / demographic structure / demographic balance / population size / population density |
| the population was 5 million | the demographic figure stood at 5 million / the population reached 5 million |
| population grew | the population expanded / experienced demographic growth |
| population shrank | the population contracted / underwent demographic decline |
| birth rate | fertility rate / natality |
| death rate | mortality rate |
| ageing population | greying population / demographic ageing |
| densely populated | densely inhabited / heavily settled |

```
- The demographic figure of São Paulo stood at 10.2 million in 1980, rising to 18.4 million by 2015.
- China underwent significant demographic ageing between 2000 and 2020.
- The population density of urban areas was considerably higher than that of rural counterparts.
```

## 5. Year / time / period

🇨🇳 instructor rule (zhihu p/83528343): always paraphrase time twice — once in the intro, once in the overview. Do not write `from 2000 to 2010` four times.

| Common | Upgrade |
|---|---|
| from 2000 to 2010 | between 2000 and 2010 / over the decade beginning in 2000 / over the ten-year period from 2000 to 2010 / during the 10-year span |
| in 2000 | in the year 2000 / by 2000 / as of 2000 |
| over the years | over the period / over time / across the timeframe / throughout the period |
| 30 years ago | three decades ago / in the preceding 30 years |
| in the past | in earlier years / historically / in prior decades |
| in the future | in the years to come / in coming decades / going forward |
| nowadays | at present / in current times / in modern times |
| recently | in recent years / of late / in the recent past |
| during this period | over this span / throughout this interval / within this timeframe |
| every year | annually / on a yearly basis / per annum |

```
- Over the three-decade span from 1980 to 2010, the figures rose steadily.
- As of 2015, the proportion stood at approximately 45%.
- Throughout the timeframe presented, the trend remained largely upward.
```

## 6. Number / amount / proportion / percentage

🇨🇳 (zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37521466): the number-vs-amount-vs-proportion confusion is the single most common L1 Chinese error in Task 1.

| Common | Upgrade |
|---|---|
| the number of (countable) | the figure for / how many / the count of |
| the amount of (uncountable) | the volume of / how much / the quantity of |
| percentage | proportion / share / fraction / percent |
| 50% | half / one-half / a half |
| 25% | a quarter / one-quarter / a fourth |
| 33% | a third / one-third |
| 75% | three-quarters / three-fourths |
| 10% | one in ten / a tenth / one-tenth |
| 20% | a fifth / one in five |
| 70% | seven out of ten / seven in ten |
| approximately 50% | around half / roughly half / approximately one in two |
| just over 25% | slightly more than a quarter / just above a quarter |
| just under 25% | slightly less than a quarter / just below a quarter |
| slightly less than half | almost half / nearly half / just under 50% |
| more than half | a majority / over half / the larger share |
| less than half | a minority / under half / the smaller share |

🇯🇵 (solo-ielts-toefl.com) example pairings the JP cram-school recommends:

| Specific number | Soft phrasing |
|---|---|
| 22% | just over a fifth |
| 23% | slightly less than a quarter |
| 48% | approximately half |
| 70% | a large proportion |
| 80% | a significant proportion / the vast majority |
| 5–10% | a small minority |

## 10. "Compare" / "compared to"

| Common | Upgrade |
|---|---|
| compared to X | in contrast to X / in comparison with X / relative to X / as opposed to X / vis-à-vis X |
| same as | comparable to / on a par with / equivalent to / identical to |
| different from | divergent from / at odds with / in contrast with |
| while | whereas / by contrast / on the other hand / conversely |

⚠ instructor-flagged error (CN earthedu.com): do **not** write `compare X to Y` when showing differences — use `contrast X with Y`. `Compare to` = similarities; `contrast with` = differences.

## 11. Subject-paraphrase frames (Task 1 specifics)

🇨🇳 instructor canon (zim.vn / xhd.cn): the subject is where students get stuck. Pre-built subject substitutions:

| Original prompt subject | Subject paraphrase |
|---|---|
| spending on X | the expenditure on X / the amount allocated to X / the budget devoted to X |
| sales of X | the turnover of X / the volume of X sold / the amount of X purchased |
| production of X | the output of X / the manufacture of X / the quantity of X produced |
| consumption of X | the use of X / the volume of X consumed / X usage rates |
| means of transport | modes / forms / types of transportation / vehicles |
| car owners | car users / motorists / drivers / those who commute by car |
| Internet users | those connected to the Internet / online users / individuals with Internet access |
| how people in X spent time | the way time was spent by people in X / the manner in which time was allocated by individuals in X |

```
Original:  "how people in Japan spent time reading"
Upgraded:  "how time was spent on reading by Japanese citizens"
           "the manner in which Japanese individuals allocated time to reading"
```

## 12. "How much / how many" structure (CN cram-school favourite)

🇨🇳 zim.vn rule: replace `the amount/number of X` with `how much/how many X + S + V` for grammatical range.

| Original | Restructure |
|---|---|
| the amount of waste an individual produced | how much waste an individual produced |
| the number of cars owned by households | how many cars households owned |
| the percentage of women in the workforce | what proportion of the workforce was female |
| the consumption of energy by sector | how energy was consumed across sectors |

## 15. Quick-reference cheat sheet for Task 1 introduction paraphrase

🇻🇳 ZIM rule — three components must change in the intro:

1. **The verb** (`shows` → `illustrates / depicts / outlines`)
2. **The time** (`from 2000 to 2010` → `over the decade beginning in 2000`)
3. **The subject noun** (`population` → `demographic figure`; `people` → `citizens`)

```
Prompt:    "The graph below shows population figures for India and China since 2000."
Band 6:    "The graph shows the population in India and China since 2000."  (≈ copied)
Band 7:    "The supplied line graph illustrates the demographic figures of two
            nations, India and China, over the period beginning in the year 2000."
```
