# Paraphrase Quick Lookup — Alphabetical (200+ rows)

> The "panic lookup" reference. When you have written a band-5/6 word and need a band-7 swap *now*, find the row, pick from the alternatives, and check the collocation tip. Each row gives 3–5 academic alternatives + a collocation hint so you don't pick a synonym that doesn't fit the slot.
>
> Pair with `02-vocabulary-error-correction-bank.md` (categorised + reasons) and `contexts/05-band-6-to-7/vocabulary-upgrades.md` (strategy + topic clusters).
>
> **Format:** word | alternatives | collocation tip. **Caveat:** synonyms shift meaning; always re-read the sentence after substituting.
