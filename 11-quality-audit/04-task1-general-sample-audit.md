## Roll-up across Task 1 General Training

| Verdict | Count | % |
|---|---|---|
| Broadly accurate | ≈12 of 13 (real or model) | ~92% |
| Likely inflated on per-criterion only | 1 | — |
| Composite / non-auditable | ≈15 of 31 (~48% of file content) | — |
| Likely deflated | 0 | — |

**The dominant issue with the GT file set is composite content, not score inflation.** ~half of "samples" are typical-pattern descriptions, not real candidate writing. Where real samples exist, they are well-calibrated, and the per-criterion scores from writing9 here are mostly sensible (in contrast to Task 1 Academic where writing9 per-criterion is broken).

**The single per-criterion outlier is Sample 1 in Band 7.0** (LR=9 / GRA=9 with "could elaborate" feedback), which exhibits the writing9 algorithmic-scoring distortion seen in Task 1 Academic.

**Model-vs-candidate confusion is highest in the Band 7.0 file:** ~5 of 10 samples are Band 8/9 models being held up as Band 7+ targets. Real Band 7 letters (Samples 1, 4, 5) are quite different from these models.
